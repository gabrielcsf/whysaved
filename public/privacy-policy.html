Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Why Saved ("the Extension") is a Chrome extension that helps you save bookmarks with context so you remember why you saved them. This policy explains what data the Extension collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Data We Collect

The Extension collects only what you explicitly provide:

  • Page URL and title — read from the active tab when you open the popup, to pre-fill the bookmark form.
  • Favicon — the icon of the page you are bookmarking, fetched from the browser.
  • Your note — the reason or context you type before saving a bookmark.
  • AI-generated summary and tags — created from the URL, title, and note you provided, using a third-party AI service.

The Extension does not collect browsing history, track background activity, or access any page content.

2. How Data Is Used

  • Your bookmarks (URL, title, note, favicon, summary, tags) are stored in a cloud database (Supabase) linked to your account, so you can view and search them later.
  • Your URL, title, and note are sent to an AI service to generate a short summary and tags. No other data is sent to that service.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored in Supabase, a secure cloud database. Row-level security ensures each user can only access their own bookmarks.
  • Data is transmitted over HTTPS at all times.
  • We do not sell, share, or rent your data to any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication. Privacy policy.
  • OpenRouter — AI inference for bookmark enrichment. Privacy policy.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your bookmarks are retained until you delete them or request account deletion. To delete all your data, contact us at the email below.

6. Permissions

The Extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab — read the URL, title, and favicon of the current tab when you open the popup.
  • storage — store extension settings locally.
  • tabs — access tab metadata needed to pre-fill the bookmark form.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the Extension evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
